# Business Readiness Rules

## Purpose

This file defines what it means for a business surface to be ready for stronger use inside the application plane.

It exists so the platform can distinguish between a business that is merely described and a business that is ready for review, comparison, onboarding influence, or promotion thinking.

This is the readiness-rules layer of the business-framework governance branch.

## Why This Exists

A business may be complete enough to understand, but still not ready for stronger platform use.

Readiness should mean that the business can be used in a disciplined way by surrounding systems.

## Readiness Conditions

A business surface is more likely to be ready when:

### 1. Minimum Definition Exists
The business is no longer only named.

### 2. Core Meaning Is Stable
Its purpose, primary value, and stakeholder picture are not changing wildly from one pass to another.

### 3. Shared Framework Mapping Exists
The business can be interpreted through shared framework elements such as:

- perspectives
- communication structures
- canvases
- building blocks

### 4. Structural Boundaries Are Clear
The platform can tell:

- what belongs to the business
- what belongs to tenant expression
- what belongs to operations
- what belongs outside the business plane

### 5. Review Use Is Possible
A reviewer can inspect the business and answer meaningful questions without guessing too much.

### 6. Comparison Use Is Possible
The business is described clearly enough to be compared against another business surface without forced interpretation.

## Readiness Levels

### Early
Useful for thought and exploration, but not yet dependable.

### Emerging
Strong enough for review and iterative refinement.

### Ready
Strong enough to support structured comparison and governance use.

### Mature
Strong enough to influence onboarding, promotion, and future automation logic.

## Important Note

Readiness is not perfection.

Readiness means the business has enough stability and structure to support disciplined platform use.

## Final Principle

A ready business can be carried by the system, not only admired by the author.

This file exists so readiness becomes visible before stronger control is applied.
