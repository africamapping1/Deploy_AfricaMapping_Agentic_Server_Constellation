# Business Completeness Rules

## Purpose

This file defines how the platform should think about business completeness inside the business framework.

It exists so a business can be judged as more than merely present.

This is the completeness-rules layer of the business-framework governance branch.

## Why This Exists

A business may be:

- named
- partially described
- strongly framed
- broadly modeled
- operationally connected

These are not the same thing.

The platform needs a way to recognize how complete a business surface really is.

## Completeness Levels

### Level 0 — Named Only
The business has a name or folder presence, but little meaningful explanation.

### Level 1 — Minimally Defined
The business satisfies the minimum business definition and can be recognized as a real business surface.

### Level 2 — Structurally Described
The business includes clear structure such as:

- purpose
- stakeholders
- value
- business components
- internal organization or surfaces

### Level 3 — Framework-Aligned
The business is described using shared framework elements such as:

- perspectives
- communication structures
- canvases
- business-model building blocks

### Level 4 — Tenant-Comparable
The business is described in a way that supports comparison across tenants or business instances.

### Level 5 — Operationally Useful
The business definition is strong enough to inform:

- review
- onboarding
- planning
- prioritization
- governance discussion

### Level 6 — Control-Ready
The business definition is stable and structured enough to be evaluated directly by the Service Availability Matrix.

## Main Rule

Completeness is not only about quantity of files.

It is about whether the business can be:

- understood
- compared
- reviewed
- carried forward

## Important Note

A business can be rich in words and still incomplete if its structure is unclear.

A business can also be concise and still complete enough if its meaning is well organized.

## Final Principle

Business completeness means usable structure, not only document volume.

This file exists so completeness can be judged more honestly.
