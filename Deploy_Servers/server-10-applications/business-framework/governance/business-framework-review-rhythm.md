# Business Framework Review Rhythm

## Purpose

This file defines the review rhythm that should help keep the business framework healthy over time.

It exists so the framework does not grow only by bursts of writing and then remain untouched.

This is the review-rhythm layer of the business-framework governance branch.

## Why This Exists

A business framework can decay quietly when:

- definitions drift
- summaries age
- patterns multiply without discipline
- different business areas evolve unevenly

A regular review rhythm helps reduce this.

## Review Rhythm Types

### Light Review
Used for:
- checking freshness
- spotting stale summaries
- spotting naming drift
- identifying obvious gaps

### Structural Review
Used for:
- checking framework coherence
- checking overlap between components
- checking whether categories still make sense

### Maturity Review
Used for:
- judging completeness
- judging readiness
- identifying promotion candidates
- identifying weak comparison surfaces

## Suggested Rhythm

The exact cadence may vary, but the platform should aim for repeated review rather than rare heroic cleanup.

A healthy rhythm usually includes:

- light review more often
- structural review less often
- maturity review at meaningful checkpoints

## Important Note

A framework review should not only criticize.

It should also confirm what is now stable and useful.

## Final Principle

A framework remains healthy when review becomes habit rather than rescue.

This file exists so business structure can evolve with discipline.
