# Foundations

## Purpose

This folder contains the main business-understanding surfaces of the business framework.

It exists so the platform can organize the reusable ways of understanding a business before those ideas are expressed, governed, or applied to tenant-specific application spaces.

## Contents

### `business-model/`
Structural business truth.

This is where the framework captures the core components that make up a business model.

### `canvases/`
Whole-business views.

This is where the framework captures compact views that help see the business as a whole.

### `perspectives/`
Lens-based views.

This is where the framework captures different angles from which the business may be understood.

## Final Principle

Foundations are for understanding what the business is.
