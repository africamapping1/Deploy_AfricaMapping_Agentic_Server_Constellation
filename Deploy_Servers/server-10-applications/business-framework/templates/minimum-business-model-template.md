# Minimum Business Model Template

## Purpose

This template provides the minimum business-model structure that a tenant should eventually fill.

It exists so new tenants do not begin with only names and broad ideas, but can be guided toward a comparable and reviewable business shape.

This is a template, not a completed business model.

## Completion Check

This template is more useful when all sections are filled at least at a basic level.

A partially filled template may still be useful, but should be treated as:

- early
- incomplete
- not yet strongly comparable

---

## Final Principle

A tenant becomes easier to compare, review, and strengthen when its business model is expressed through shared structure.

This template exists so future tenants can start from that shared structure.
