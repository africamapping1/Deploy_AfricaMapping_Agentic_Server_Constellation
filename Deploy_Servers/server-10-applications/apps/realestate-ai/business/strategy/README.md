# RealEstate AI Strategy

## Purpose

This directory contains the tenant-specific strategy layer for `realestate-ai`.

It is where the business defines direction, priorities, growth logic, and decision posture.

This is not the business model itself.

This is not communications.

This is the directional layer that guides how the business evolves.

## Why This Exists

A business needs more than structure.

It also needs direction.

`realestate-ai` should be able to define:

- what it is trying to become
- what it should prioritize next
- what capabilities matter most
- what risks should be managed
- what growth paths are realistic
- what decisions should be favored or delayed

This directory exists so the business can think strategically rather than only operationally.

## Relationship to the Framework

The reusable business framework provides general structure.

This tenant strategy directory provides the real direction for `realestate-ai`.

It translates business understanding into business movement.
