# RealEstate AI Business

## Purpose

This directory contains the tenant-specific business definition for `realestate-ai`.

It is where the generic business framework becomes a real business model, real business narrative, and real business operating picture for this tenant.

This is not platform doctrine.

This is not runtime hosting.

This is the painted tenant business canvas.

## Why This Exists

The reusable business framework provides structure.

But a tenant business needs its own specific reality.

For `realestate-ai`, that means the system should be able to represent:

- what the business is trying to achieve
- who the business serves
- what value it provides
- how it evaluates properties
- how it communicates with customers and employees
- how it explains itself to investors, partners, and operators
- how it evolves over time

This directory is the tenant’s business truth layer.

## Relationship to the Framework

`server-10-applications/business-framework/` is the reusable blank canvas.

`apps/realestate-ai/business/` is the tenant-specific version painted with real business meaning.

The framework provides structure.

This directory provides the content.
