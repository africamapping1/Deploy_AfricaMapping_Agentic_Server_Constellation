# AfricaMapping Business Model

## Purpose

This file applies the minimum business-model template to the AfricaMapping tenant.

It exists so the AfricaMapping business can be expressed in a structured, comparable, and reviewable form.

---

## Tenant

- Tenant: africamapping
- Profile: geospatial platform / mapping infrastructure / address system
- Date:
- Status: early to emerging

---

## 1. Customer or Stakeholder Segments

- Primary customers or stakeholders:
  - local governments
  - mapping professionals
  - GIS users across Africa

- Secondary customers or stakeholders:
  - private companies needing location data
  - logistics and transportation services

- Internal users:
  - platform operators
  - data managers

- External users:
  - public users accessing maps or addresses
  - organizations consuming mapping data

- Important dependencies:
  - geospatial data providers
  - ArcGIS Online or similar systems
  - local authorities

---

## 2. Value Proposition

- Core value provided:
  - structured, accessible mapping and address data for Africa

- Main problem solved:
  - lack of standardized, accessible, and usable mapping/address systems

- Why this matters:
  - supports planning, logistics, governance, and data-driven decision-making

- What makes this tenant meaningful:
  - combines mapping, addressing, and platform thinking in one system
