# AfricaMapping Application Space

## Purpose

AfricaMapping is the first business consumer of the constellation's application layer.

It consumes infrastructure and applications to become operational without having to manage platform internals directly.

## Current Known Business Assets

- domain name
- website
- QuickBooks
- ArcGIS Online account

## Planned / Needed Capabilities

- CRM
- social media presence
- project tracking
- program management
- people visibility
- activity tracking
- operational coordination

## Delivery Model

The constellation delivers the operational vehicle.
AfricaMapping drives it.
