# Flow 01 - Monitoring Role

## Role

Observation, evidence gathering, alerting, and audit preservation.

## Receives

- bastion verification events
- app ingestion logs
- db write events
- error and health signals

## Performs

- classify events
- record metrics
- detect anomalies
- preserve audit evidence
- trigger alerts when thresholds are crossed

## Produces

- health signals
- incident records
- dashboards
- audit trail

## Sends To

- human operators
- governance and review processes
- historical observability records

## Failure Path

If abnormal patterns are detected, alerts are raised and evidence is preserved for investigation and future governance review.
